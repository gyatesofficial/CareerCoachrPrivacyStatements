# Privacy Policy

**CareerBuddy**
**Last Updated: December 18, 2025**

## Introduction

CareerBuddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information**
- **Email Address**: Collected through Sign in with Apple for account creation and communication
- **Name**: First and last name provided through Sign in with Apple for personalization

**User Content**
- **Resume Data**: When you upload your resume (PDF or DOCX), we extract and analyze text content including your work history, skills, education, and projects
- **Career Goals**: Text you enter describing your career aspirations and objectives
- **Notes and Tracking Data**: Information you add when marking skills, projects, or actions as complete

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Identifiers**
- **User ID**: A unique identifier created when you sign up, used to associate your data with your account

**Purchase Information**
- **Subscription Status**: Whether you have an active subscription or lifetime purchase
- **Purchase History**: Records of in-app purchases processed through Apple's App Store

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- Location data
- Photos or camera access
- Contacts or address book
- Browsing history
- Health or fitness data
- Financial information (payment processing is handled entirely by Apple)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Core Services**: Analyze your resume, generate personalized career roadmaps, and track your progress
- **Personalization**: Create AI-powered learning plans and milestone recommendations based on your profile and goals
- **Account Management**: Authenticate your identity and maintain your account
- **Product Improvement**: Understand how users interact with the App to improve features and user experience
- **Communication**: Send important updates about your account or the App (we do not send marketing emails)

## AI Processing and Third-Party Services

### OpenAI
We use OpenAI's GPT-4 API to:
- Analyze resume content and extract skills
- Generate personalized career roadmaps
- Create learning plans for skills and projects

**Important**: Your resume content and career goals are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. Per OpenAI's API data usage policy, data submitted through the API is not used to train their models. OpenAI retains API data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, after which it is deleted.

### Firebase (Google)
We use Firebase services for:
- **Authentication**: Secure sign-in via Sign in with Apple
- **Cloud Firestore**: Store your profile, skills, projects, milestones, and progress
- **Cloud Storage**: Store your uploaded resume files
- **Cloud Functions**: Process resume analysis and roadmap generation

Firebase data is stored in the United States. For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Apple
- **Sign in with Apple**: Authentication is processed by Apple
- **App Store**: All purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using Firebase's cloud infrastructure
- Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
- Data at rest is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- Access to user data is restricted to authenticated users only
- We implement Firebase Security Rules to ensure users can only access their own data

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained as long as your account is active
- **Resume Files**: Stored until you delete your account
- **Usage Data**: Retained for the lifetime of your account
- **Deleted Accounts**: When you request account deletion, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Portability
You can view all your data within the App, including your profile, skills, projects, and milestones.

### Correction
You can update your career goals and other editable information directly in the App.

### Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

### Opt-Out
- You can stop using the App at any time
- You can manage notification preferences in your device settings

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States where our servers are located. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
